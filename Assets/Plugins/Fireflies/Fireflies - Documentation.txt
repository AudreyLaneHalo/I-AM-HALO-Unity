A cloud of flying/glowing fireflies to add atmosphere to your environment.

Simply load the example Fireflies.unity scene, or drag Fireflies from the Prefabs folder into the Hierarchy window.

Additionally, add the Forest_Audio prefab to your scene for sound effects.

Have fun!
